How to install the landing page template on your hosting

1. Download the landing page archive.
2. Unzip the archive.
3. Connect to your hosting via FTP or the control panel.
4. Upload the landing page files to the public_html folder.
5. Add your tracking links and update the Meta title & description in the content.
6. Open your browser and go to: https://your-domain.com

If everything is set up correctly, your landing page should load without errors.
